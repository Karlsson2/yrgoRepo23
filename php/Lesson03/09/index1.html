Bonus Family

Blended families are becoming more common as people increasingly get divorced and remarried. This Swedish drama explores the complexities of relationships when two families come together as one. When Lisa and Patrik, who both have children with their exes, get married difficulties arise with everyone living together under one roof. The new family dynamic sometimes leads to problems with what should be a typical task, such as throwing a joint birthday party for their two sons. And then there are the exes, who remain in Lisa and Patrik's life.
